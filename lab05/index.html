Lab05 Reflection – Best Design Practices

October 5, 2025 • Topic: Responsive Web Design

In my perspective, it should start with a single-column layout that looks good on a mobile screen. Use comfortable spacing, and to make it suitable for bigger screens, one media query should be added. It should also ensure that the colors have the best contrast. Place the navigation menu where it is easy to reach at the top, and the pages can be resized by the browser or viewed on devices like phones and tablets.

Why Responsive Web Design Matters

Responsive design is one of the most important design approaches that works seamlessly across different devices such as phones, tablets, and desktops. It helps improve performance, usability, and the overall experience for all users. Another important benefit is that it reduces the need to maintain separate pages for different devices.
